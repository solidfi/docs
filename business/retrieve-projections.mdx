---
title: "Retrieve Business Projections"
description: API to get projected amounts of annual transaction volume in each of the payment form factors
---
Use this API to retrieve estimated annual transaction projections for a business.

```url ENDPOINT
GET /v1/business/{{businessId}}/projection
```
