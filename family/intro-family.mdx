title: "Introduction to Family"
description: Family is a group of one or more parents, their children or any other individuals.
---

A Family is an association of multiple persons in a program for the purposes of creating accounts and issuing cards to family members. For more information, [see the Help Center article](https://help.solidfi.com/hc/en-us/articles/6957483290523-Family-Accounts).

**Family** has:  
- person that is the creator  
- one or more members (other persons)
